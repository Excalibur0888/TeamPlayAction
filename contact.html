Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a fantasy basketball league?

To join a league, you'll need to create an account first. Once you're logged in, you can either join a public league or create your own private league and invite friends to join.

Is TeamPlayAction free to use?

We offer both free and premium tiers. Basic fantasy basketball leagues are free to play, while premium features and specialized league formats require a subscription.

How quickly do stats update during games?

Our platform updates player statistics in near real-time, with delays typically under 60 seconds from live game action.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes! TeamPlayAction is fully responsive and works on all devices. We also offer dedicated iOS and Android apps for an enhanced mobile experience.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can use the contact form on this page, or email us directly at contact@teamplayaction.com with your feedback.
